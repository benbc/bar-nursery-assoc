About the Bar Nursery Association

Women are leaving the independent Bar in disproportionate numbers after having children. This is a matter of real concern, not only as regards equal access to and diversity of the Bar, but also for the achievement of those aims in the Judiciary. Part of the solution would be childcare provision within or near the Inns of Court.

The Bar Nursery Association is an unincorporated association established in 2007 which aims to:

  • Encourage the retention of women barristers at the Bar in the interests of equality and diversity
  • Promote the establishment childcare facilities in or near the Inns of Court
  • Improve the quality of life of barristers with young families

The Patrons of the Bar Nursery Association are the Lord Chief Justice, Baroness Hale, Lord Mance, Lady Justice Arden, Lord Walker, Mrs Justice Cox and Edwin Glasgow QC. The Bar Nursery Association is also supported by the Bar Council, the Young Bar Committee and the Association of Women Barristers.

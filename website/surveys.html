Our surveys

The Bar Nursery Association has conducted four internet surveys.

The fourth survey, which can be completed by clicking here, seeks to ascertain interest in the proposed Middle Temple nursery from potential users.

The third survey, sought to find out whether support for a nursery would affect the decisions of law students about pupillage and joining a particular Inn.

The second survey, aimed to obtain a more detailed picture of the likely demand for the proposed nursery, to assist in creating a realistic business plan.

The first survey assessed support for the principle of nursery facilities in or near the Inns of Court. 450 people responded, and almost everyone was very enthusiastic about the idea.

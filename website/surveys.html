Our surveys

The Bar Nursery Association has conducted three internet surveys.

The first survey assessed support for the principle of nursery facilities in or near the Inns of Court. 450 people responded, and almost everyone was very enthusiastic about the idea.

The second survey, which can be completed by clicking here, aims to obtain a more detailed picture of the likely demand for the proposed nursery, to assist in creating a realistic business plan.

The third survey, which can be completed by clicking here, seeks to find out whether support for a nursery would affect the decisions of law students about pupillage and joining a particular Inn.

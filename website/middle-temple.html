Proposed Middle Temple Nursery

The BNA is putting a proposal to Middle Temple to use the site currently occupied by the St Clement's Cafe to a nursery provider. The nursery would be open from 7am to 7pm Monday to Friday and would provide high quality childcare for around 40 children aged 0 to 5.

The Middle Temple cafe site is the first location that has become available in over two years that is suitable for the nursery. The site is more than suitable: it is ideal. It has all the features required for a successful Bar Nursery.

The Bar Nursery Association strongly believes that this is the best opportunity to establish a nursery that is likely to arise in the medium term, and that if the proposal is rejected by Middle Temple, the prospects of there being a Bar Nursery in the next 10 years are remote.

The proposed nursery would provide full time and part time places as well as emergency care. The BNA envisages that some chambers would purchase hours of emergency care in advance for use by their members and employees, and that individuals may also want to purchase advance emergency care. It may be that priority will be offered to members and employees of Middle Temple. Further details about the proposed nursery can be obtained by contacting the BNA (admin@barnurseryassociation.org.uk).

Pledges

The nursery will require initial investment to refurbish the site and make it suitable and safe for children. It will not be able to compete with the commercial rent that Middle Temple could obtain were the space to be used for offices. The BNA is therefore proposing to Middle Temple that if the lease is granted, the BNA will seek to raise £200,000 to support the initial costs of establishing the nursery and compensate Middle Temple for the financial implications of supporting the nursery instead of other potential uses for the space.

If you support the Middle Temple proposal, please make a pledge to help us reach our £200,000 target. We are seeking pledges from individuals and chambers, which are conditional on Middle Temple agreeing to lease the site to a nursery provider.

To make a pledge, please email the BNA (admin@barnurseryassociation.org.uk) with your name, email address and the amount of money you agree to pledge. If the proposal is successful, we envisage that the pledges will be converted to donations which will be made to Middle Temple and will therefore be charitable donations.
